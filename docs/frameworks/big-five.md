# Big Five Personality Framework Reference

## Overview

The Big Five (also called OCEAN) is a research-backed personality model measuring five broad dimensions. Each dimension is a spectrum, not a binary.

## Dimensions

### Openness to Experience (O)
- **High:** Curious, imaginative, drawn to novelty, appreciates art and abstract ideas
- **Low:** Practical, conventional, prefers routine, concrete over abstract
- **Card pair examples:**
  - "A weekend exploring somewhere new" / "A weekend in your favorite familiar spot"
  - "You enjoy ideas even if they have no practical use" / "You prefer ideas you can apply right away"

### Conscientiousness (C)
- **High:** Organized, disciplined, plans ahead, detail-oriented
- **Low:** Spontaneous, flexible, improvises, comfortable with ambiguity
- **Card pair examples:**
  - "You plan before you act" / "You figure it out as you go"
  - "A clear schedule makes you feel free" / "An open day makes you feel free"

### Extraversion (E)
- **High:** Energized by people, talkative, seeks stimulation, action-oriented
- **Low:** Energized by solitude, reserved, reflective, prefers depth over breadth
- **Card pair examples:**
  - "You recharge around other people" / "You recharge with time alone"
  - "You think by talking things through" / "You think by sitting with it quietly"

### Agreeableness (A)
- **High:** Cooperative, trusting, empathetic, conflict-averse
- **Low:** Direct, skeptical, competitive, values honesty over harmony
- **Card pair examples:**
  - "You prioritize harmony in disagreements" / "You prioritize honesty in disagreements"
  - "You tend to trust people first" / "You tend to evaluate people first"

### Neuroticism (N)
- **High:** Emotionally reactive, experiences stress intensely, mood variability
- **Low:** Emotionally stable, calm under pressure, even-keeled
- **Card pair examples:**
  - "Your mood shifts easily with circumstances" / "Your mood stays steady regardless"
  - "You feel things deeply and quickly" / "You process things slowly and evenly"

## Scoring for Selfscape

Each dimension has 2 card pairs (10 total cards). Each swipe scores +1 or -1 on the relevant dimension.

| Score | Label | Display Term (example) |
|-------|-------|----------------------|
| -2 | Low | Grounded / Practical / Independent / etc. |
| -1 | Moderate-Low | Leaning [low descriptor] |
| 0 | Moderate | Balanced |
| +1 | Moderate-High | Leaning [high descriptor] |
| +2 | High | Boundlessly curious / Deeply structured / etc. |

## Evocative Display Labels (not clinical)

| Dimension | High | Low |
|-----------|------|-----|
| Openness | Boundlessly curious | Deeply grounded |
| Conscientiousness | Deliberately structured | Freely spontaneous |
| Extraversion | Energized by connection | Powered by solitude |
| Agreeableness | Naturally trusting | Fiercely independent |
| Neuroticism | Deeply feeling | Unshakably steady |

## Visual Mapping (for compositing layer)

| Dimension | High Effect | Low Effect |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Openness | More saturated colors, surreal/fantastical elements | Muted palette, structured/orderly elements |
| Conscientiousness | Sharper edges, defined geometry, clean lines | Softer edges, organic shapes, flowing forms |
| Extraversion | Warmer tones, more populated scene, brighter | Cooler tones, more solitary, dimmer |
| Agreeableness | Softer overall tone, harmonious palette | Higher contrast, more dramatic lighting |
| Neuroticism | More atmospheric variation, moodier sky | Even, stable lighting, calm atmosphere |
